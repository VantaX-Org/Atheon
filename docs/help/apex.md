# Apex - Business Health

The Apex layer provides a 360-degree view of your organization's operational health.

## Key Concepts
- **Health Score**: Composite metric (0-100) measuring overall business wellness.
- **Risk Alerts**: Automated detection of anomalous patterns.
- **Briefings**: AI-generated executive summaries.

## Common Actions
- Review daily health score trends
- Drill into specific dimension scores
- Acknowledge and assign risk alerts
- Generate board-ready reports
