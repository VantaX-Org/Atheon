# Product

## Register

product

## Users

C-suite and finance operators at mid-to-large enterprises (Board member, CEO, CFO, COO, CPO, Financial Controller, Finance Manager, Accounts Payable, Tax & Compliance, Operations Manager). They arrive between meetings, on desktop, wanting one answer fast: how much money is leaking, what was recovered, what needs a decision today. Secondary surface: prospects landing on the marketing page (`/`) deciding whether to book an assessment.

## Product Purpose

Atheon finds money enterprises are leaking (duplicate payments, pricing errors, missed rebates, tax over/under-payment), recovers it, and proves every claim with a sealed evidence ledger. Success = a CFO trusts a number on screen enough to act on it without asking for a spreadsheet. Business model: no recovery, no fee.

## Brand Personality

Grounded, exact, calm. The product speaks like a forensic accountant, not a growth marketer. Emotional goal: relief and confidence — "someone finally has receipts." The brand river (flowing particle stream) is the one expressive element; everything else earns trust through precision.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboard slop: gradient hero-metrics, glassmorphism cards, identical icon-card grids, uppercase tracked eyebrows on every section.
- Fabricated proof: invented testimonials, customer-count logos, made-up zeros. A dash means "source hasn't reported"; nothing is ever faked (honesty law).
- Analyst-deck density: comparison matrices and manifesto walls were deliberately deleted from marketing.

## Design Principles

1. **Honesty is layout.** Unreported data renders as "—", never 0. Illustrative money figures are labeled as such. The UI must be able to say "I don't know."
2. **One screen, one decision.** Fold tabs and admin sprawl into single purposeful screens (console quarantine); every persona lands on the answer for their role.
3. **The river carries the brand.** Motion and 3D live in the river motif (login, marketing, console); everywhere else motion is functional and reduced-motion-safe.
4. **Live or nothing.** No mock, fallback, or hardcoded data anywhere; demo tenant (Vantax) is seeded with real period data so every number resolves live.
5. **Evidence over adjectives.** Sealed hashes, provenance chains, and verified stats (445+ checks, 75+ connectors, 50+ jurisdictions) instead of marketing claims.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA target: ≥4.5:1 body contrast, visible focus states, semantic landmarks. Reduced motion respected at three layers (skip 3D mounts, MotionConfig reducedMotion="user", CSS opt-outs). Keyboard-first navigation on all console surfaces.
